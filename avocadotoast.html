Avocado Toast

Description

A delicious breakfast lunch or dinner depending on which time

of day you enjoy

Ingredients

  • 1 Avocado
  • 2 Slice of bread (sourdough is best)
  • Salt to taste
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Chia seeds (optional)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Garlic powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast bread
  2. Mash avocado and mix with salt (add lemon juice now if desired)
  3. Spread mash liberally on toast and top with remaining optional ingredients if desired

PB&J

Description

Is it a snack? is it a meal? You decide.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly (or any other variety)
  • 2 Slice of bread (martin's potato is best)

Steps

  1. Spread PB on one slice of bread
  2. Spread Jelly on the other side of bread
  3. Fold together (cutting in half optional though encouraged) and enjoy!
